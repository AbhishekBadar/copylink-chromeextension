1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Copy URL & QR Code Generator extension does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Browsing history or website URLs
  • User behavior or analytics data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any form of personally identifiable information (PII)

2. Local Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User Preferences: Your chosen copy format settings (plain text, Markdown, HTML)
  • Copy History: Recent URLs you've copied (stored locally, never transmitted)
  • UI Settings: Position of the floating button and other interface preferences
  • Extension Configuration: Settings like history limits and notification preferences
Important: All data remains on your device. Nothing is ever sent to our servers or any third-party services.

3. Data Transmission

External Network Requests: NONE

This extension operates completely offline and makes zero external network requests:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No API calls to third-party services
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertisements or external content loading
  • QR codes are generated locally using bundled libraries
